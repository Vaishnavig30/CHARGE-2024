What is CHARGE?

About Charge CHARGE is a three-day extravaganza packed with electrifying events, workshops, and competitions designed to inspire and engage participants from all corners of the country. From hackathons to panel discussions, project expos to hands-on workshops, CHARGE offers something for everyone interested in the world of electronics and technology.

"CHARGE is not just an event; it's a catalyst for transformation, a spark that ignites the flames of innovation."

Our Mission

At CHARGE, our mission is to inspire, empower, and connect individuals who are passionate about technology and innovation. Through a series of engaging events, workshops, and competitions, we aim to foster creativity, collaboration, and lifelong learning among participants.

What We Offer

CHARGE offers a diverse range of events, including hackathons, panel discussions, workshops, and project expos, designed to cater to various interests and skill levels.

Our workshops provide participants with hands-on experience and practical knowledge in areas such as artificial intelligence, drone technology, circuit design, and more.

Test your skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities in our hackathons, quiz competitions, and project expos, and stand a chance to win exciting prizes and recognition.
